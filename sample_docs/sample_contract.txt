MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of
January 15, 2024 ("Effective Date") and shall remain in effect until
January 14, 2027 ("Expiry Date"), unless terminated earlier in accordance
with Section 8 herein.

PARTIES:

1. NovaStream Analytics, Inc., a Delaware corporation ("Disclosing Party")
   Address: 500 Market Street, San Jose, CA 95113
   Role: Technology Provider

2. Horizon Ventures Capital, LP ("Receiving Party")
   Address: 200 Park Avenue, New York, NY 10166
   Role: Investment Partner

GOVERNING LAW: This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in
accordance with the laws of the State of California, without regard to
conflict of law principles.

KEY OBLIGATIONS:

- Each party shall maintain the confidentiality of all Proprietary
  Information received from the other party.
- Neither party shall disclose Proprietary Information to third parties
  without prior written consent.
- Each party shall use Proprietary Information solely for evaluating a
  potential business relationship.
- Upon termination, each party shall return or destroy all Proprietary
  Information within 30 days.

PENALTY CLAUSES:

- Unauthorized disclosure shall result in liquidated damages of $250,000
  per incident.
- Injunctive relief may be sought without posting bond for any breach.
- The breaching party shall reimburse all legal fees incurred by the
  non-breaching party.
